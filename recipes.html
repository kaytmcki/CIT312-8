Iced Matcha Americano

Refresh yourself with an iced twist on the classic Americano. Whisk 1 teaspoon of bright green matcha with 2 ounces of hot water (80°C) until frothy. Allow the mixture to cool slightly, then pour it over a glass filled with ice. Top off with cold water or coconut water for a hydrating finish, and stir gently before enjoying.

Iced Matcha Latte

For a refreshing iced matcha latte, sift 3–5g of high-quality matcha powder into a bowl. Add 2 ounces of hot water (80°C) and whisk until smooth and frothy. Allow the matcha mixture to cool, then combine with 150 ml of cold milk (or your preferred milk alternative). Pour over ice in a glass, stir gently, and enjoy your cool, revitalizing drink!

Traditional Usucha

Experience the authentic Japanese tea ceremony with ususha (thin tea). Sift 1 teaspoon of matcha into a chawan (tea bowl), then pour 70–80ml of hot water (around 80°C). Using a chasen (bamboo whisk), whisk briskly until a light froth forms. The chawan enhances the aroma and texture, while the chasen ensures a smooth, well-aerated brew. Enjoy this delicate and mindful preparation.
